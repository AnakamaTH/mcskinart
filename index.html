How to Use

  1. Upload your base skin (64×64 PNG, optional).
  2. Upload your image map. It will be resized to 72×24 if needed.
  3. Click "Generate" to create 27 Minecraft skins.
  4. Equip skins skin_1 to skin_26 from the Minecraft skin page.
  5. Wait for NameMC to update before uploading the next one.
