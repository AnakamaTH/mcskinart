NameMC SkinArt Generator

How to Use

  1. Upload your base skin (optional, 64×64 PNG).
  2. Upload your map image (art). It will be resized to 72×24 if needed.
  3. Click "Generate" to create 27 Minecraft skins.
  4. Equip skins from skin_1 to skin_26 via the Minecraft website.
  5. Wait until the skin shows on NameMC before uploading the next one!
